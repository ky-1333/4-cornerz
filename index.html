-Corner 4-

As a group, we have joined to create our own projects that is based on an artifact from the British Museum. The artifact and our personal items have relations by any means linked, physically or abstract wise.

The object that I have selected and the the artifact, are both linked by numbers, aesthetics and purpos. My object is an image with 4 people in them, one being me and three of my other closest friends. The artifact is a basket with 4 stands on the bottom. The numbers corrolate to the number of people in the picture. If one of them is not there, then the basket i unstable. I interpret the stands as trust from each of us.

Before I came to the museum, I thought it would bee boring because it was just a museum. However as we walked around and explored more of it I was more engaged with the artifacts and the the history behind them, especially the Korean room.
